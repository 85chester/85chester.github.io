Join Our Team Today

WE ARE AN EQUAL OPPORTUNITY EMPLOYER

We greatly value diversity and do not discriminate against any job applicant. We continue to develop our diversity programs every year. At 85 Chester, we identify and value talent and strongly encourage you to apply to become a part of a welcoming and diverse organization.

WE PROVIDE COMPETITIVE COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS PACKAGES

85 Chester ensures our talented employees are compensated for their hard work. We truly care about our employees life inside and outside of the workplace. You will see flexible work hours, child care systems, health benefits, and many more.





WE HAVE THE MOST COMPREHENSIVE TRAINING PROGRAMS

We make the best effort to ensure our employees are prepared for their position. Our training and development programs help to cultivate the future leaders of the organization. We understand the importance of continual development and we understand the value it gives to employees and the organization.

WE PROMOTE A POSITIVE WORK ENVIRONMENT

Our managers are highly trained. They provide constant and constructive feedback to help you become better. We want you to feel comfortable at work and comfortable with communicating upwards. We truly value employee suggestions and ideas.

WE PROVIDE REWARDS AND RECOGNITION FOR STELLAR EMPLOYEES

Every month, high-performing employees are recognized. We provide varying incentives for every recognition period, like cash, gift certificates, or extra vacation days. Not only does this encourage individual excellence, but we will additionally reward team excellence. Yearly reward ceremonies are held at our yearly company dinner.
