Meet our impressive executive team

GILBERT LAU



CEO, CFO

Gilbert is the lead of the Finance Department where appropriate funding for all department projects are derived. He ensures that the proper liquid assets are on hand each year so that the other departments may operate efficiently. He is also in charge of how the funding is sourced. Additionally, another important aspect is maintaining positive and trustworthy relationships with shareholders and debtors. Gilbert graduated from the University of Waterloo with a Bachelor of Arts in Economics and has a love for finance. He joined 85 Chester as the Finance Executive in 2015 and was unanimously elected by the Board of Directors to additionally take the role of Chief Executive Officer later in that same year. He has demonstrated excellence in leadership for 8 consecutive years in guiding the company towards profit and success.



STEPHANIE WONG



Production and Plant Capacity Executive

Stephanie oversees the manufacturing process of the best quality sensors on the market. She is responsible for ensuring cost effective and time efficient production. Similar to the rest of her team, she is dedicated to producing innovative and high quality sensors to all our customers in each market. She always ensures that production capacity can meet the continually changing market demands to capture all potential market share. Stephanie graduated from the University of Waterloo with a Bachelor of Arts in Economics. Bringing with her an impressive 10 years of experience with production in the sensors industry, she joined 85 Chester as the Production Manager in 2015. In 2016, she was appointed as the Production and Plant Capacity Executive.



ASHNA MAREDIA



Marketing Executive

Ashna manages the Marketing Department where she directs the promotional campaigns of products and ensures customer accessibility and awareness for each and every product in all markets. She is involved with continued market research initiatives to ensure that 85 Chester is developing products according to consumer needs. Ashna ensures that prices are reasonable and reflect the superior quality and innovation effort that was invested into each sensor. Ashna is currently studying at the University of Waterloo. She joined 85 Chester executive team in 2016 as the Marketing Executive.



ANDRA BERARIU



Research and Development Executive

Andra is a recent graduate from the University of Waterloo who specializes in research and distribution. Andra plans and leads each step of the product development to ensure that the company’s products are innovative and cutting-edge and that the development and implementation of research is aligned with the company’s strategic goals. Andra stays current with product trends and other external trends impacting product development in order to deliver the best products possible. Her expertise in business will ensure that the products available in the marketplace will captivate the attention of the buyer while providing reliability, innovation, and variety for the customers. She is thrilled to be part of a rapidly growing company and looks forward to applying her business knowledge in the research and development department. Andra joined the 85 Chester executive team in 2016.



ANGELA MAK



HR Executive

Angela is one of the newest executives onboard the executive team. Although this is her first experience as a the Human Resources Executive, she brings an innovative new perspective to Human Resource Management. She believes in the increasing value of creating a positive relationship with the most important part of a company: the people. With 5 years of development and training alongside the now retired HR Executive, she was more than prepared to take on this challenging position. Ever since she joined the team, productivity levels have skyrocketed and employee turnover rates have decreased. She will continue to be an integral member of the executive team and many will look forward to when she unveils the next HR initiative of 85 Chester at the 2025 Board Meeting.

WE ARE AN EQUAL OPPORTUNITY EMPLOYER

We greatly value diversity and do not discriminate against any job applicant. We continue to develop our diversity programs every year. At 85 Chester, we identify and value talent and strongly encourage you to apply to become a part of a welcoming and diverse organization.

WE PROVIDE COMPETITIVE COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS PACKAGES

85 Chester ensures our talented employees are compensated for their hard work. We truly care about our employees life inside and outside of the workplace. You will see flexible work hours, child care systems, health benefits, and many more.





WE HAVE THE MOST COMPREHENSIVE TRAINING PROGRAMS

We make the best effort to ensure our employees are prepared for their position. Our training and development programs help to cultivate the future leaders of the organization. We understand the importance of continual development and we understand the value it gives to employees and the organization.

WE PROMOTE A POSITIVE WORK ENVIRONMENT

Our managers are highly trained. They provide constant and constructive feedback to help you become better. We want you to feel comfortable at work and comfortable with communicating upwards. We truly value employee suggestions and ideas.

WE PROVIDE REWARDS AND RECOGNITION FOR STELLAR EMPLOYEES

Every month, high-performing employees are recognized. We provide varying incentives for every recognition period, like cash, gift certificates, or extra vacation days. Not only does this encourage individual excellence, but we will additionally reward team excellence. Yearly reward ceremonies are held at our yearly company dinner.




WE SUPPORT CHARITABLE ORGANIZATIONS WITHIN THE COMMUNITY

At 85 Chester, we believe in giving back to the community and getting involved with meaningful causes to help bring change to the world.Employees are welcome and encouraged to volunteer and/or participate in the company campaigns and events. 85 Chester is very conscious regarding their impact on society.
